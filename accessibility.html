The Standard of Silence

Inclusion is intentional.

At Off Day Collective, we believe that the luxury of stillness and clarity should be available to everyone. A digital "Off Day" is not restorative if the technology itself creates a barrier.

We are committed to ensuring our digital experiences are accessible to individuals of all abilities. Our current goal is to meet and exceed WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, focusing on high-contrast readability, screen-reader optimization, and keyboard-only navigation.

I. Sensory Control

Our "Atmosphere" layer is entirely optional. We provide a prominent physical toggle to ensure users can choose a silent experience, preventing unexpected audio triggers.

II. Visual Clarity

We utilize a "High-Contrast Charcoal" palette. All text elements are measured against contrast ratios to ensure legibility for users with low vision.

III. Assistive Tech

The site architecture is built with semantic HTML5. Our "Deep Exhale" transitions are designed to be respectful of screen reader focus management.

Feedback is a Gift.

If you encounter any difficulty accessing our collective, please let us know immediately. Your perspective helps us build a better sanctuary.
