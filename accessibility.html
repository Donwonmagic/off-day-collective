Off Day Collective is designed for silence, clarity, and ease. We believe that peace of mind should be accessible to everyone.

We are committed to ensuring our digital platforms are usable by all people, regardless of ability or technology. Design is not just about how things look; it is about how they function for the user.

01 // Standards

We strive to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

02 // The System

Our site utilizes semantic HTML5, high-contrast text ratios, and keyboard navigation support to ensure a seamless experience. We regularly review our "System" to eliminate barriers.

03 // Feedback

If you encounter any friction while navigating our site, or if a specific feature is not accessible to you, please contact the studio.
